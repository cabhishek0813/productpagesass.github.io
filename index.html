How do Galaxy S22 Ultra and Galaxy S23 Ultra compare?

Galaxy S23 Ultra offers the highest resolution yet on a Galaxy smartphone at 200MP, compared to S22 Ultra's 108MP. With the same 5000mAh (typical) battery as S22 Ultra, Galaxy S23 Ultra delivers up to roughly 26 hours of video playback and more efficient performance thanks to improved processing power.10,21

What advantages do Galaxy S23 Ultra cameras have?

Galaxy S23 Ultra is equipped with 5 cameras: a 12MP selfie camera, a 12MP Ultra Wide Camera, a 200MP Wide-angle Camera, a 10MP telephoto camera with 10x optical zoom and a second 10MP telephoto camera for 3x optical zoom. Low light selfies are AI enhanced for crisp detail and accurate colour. Depth mapping enables faster focusing for selfies. And portraits at night are clearly defined thanks to AI-detected depth analysis.
